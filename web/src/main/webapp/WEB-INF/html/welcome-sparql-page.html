neXtProt SPARQL endpoint

    • SPARQL endpoint

The neXtProt SPARQL endpoint is accessible at: https://sparql.nexptrot.org.
We recommend our new users to start looking at our examples together with our data model.

We are aware that those queries may be difficult to create, therefore don't hesitate to contact us if you need help!
Look also at this doc to know how to save your queries in your profile.
We also provide a more advanced interface accessible at https://snorql.nextprot.org that allows user to search in different axes (SPARQL queries can also be saved in this environment).
You may want to have a look also at the presentation Practical Introduction to SPARQL and play with the The SPARQL playground.
